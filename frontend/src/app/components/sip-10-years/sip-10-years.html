Why 10 Years is the Sweet Spot for SIP

  • Compounding kicks in: Around year 7–8, your annual gains start exceeding your annual contributions — this is where the "magic" of compounding becomes visible.
  • Market cycles covered: Over 10 years, your SIP typically rides through 1–2 full market cycles, smoothing returns through rupee cost averaging.
  • Tax-efficient: Gains held over 12 months qualify for LTCG (12.5% above ₹1.25L/year), which is significantly lower than income tax slab rates on FD interest.
  • Goal alignment: 10-year SIP aligns with major financial goals — children's education fund, home down payment, car upgrade, or emergency corpus building.

10-Year SIP: Flat vs Step-Up Comparison

A ₹5,000/month flat SIP for 10 years at 12% yields ~₹11.6 lakh. The same SIP with 10% annual step-up yields ~₹17.3 lakh — a 49% increase in final corpus with gradual contribution increases that match salary growth.

Best Mutual Fund Categories for 10-Year SIP

  • Flexi-Cap Funds: Broad diversification across market caps. Ideal for a set-and-forget 10-year approach.
  • Large & Mid Cap Funds: Balanced exposure to stable large-caps and growth-oriented mid-caps.
  • Mid-Cap Funds: Higher volatility but historically stronger 10-year returns (12–16% CAGR).
  • Nifty 50 Index Fund: Lowest-cost option with market-matching returns. Excellent for passive investors.
