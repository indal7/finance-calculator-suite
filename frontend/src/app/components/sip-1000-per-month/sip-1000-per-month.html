Why ₹1,000 SIP is Perfect for Beginners

For students, recent graduates, or anyone just starting their investment journey, ₹1,000/month is an affordable entry point. Here's why it works:

  • Low barrier to entry: ₹1,000 is less than the cost of 2–3 restaurant meals per month.
  • Build the investing habit: Consistency matters more than the amount — starting early is key.
  • Compounding accelerates: Your gains in years 15–20 will exceed your total investment of years 1–10.
  • Easy to step up: As your salary grows, increase your SIP by 10% annually for dramatically better returns.

₹1,000 SIP with Step-Up — The Wealth Multiplier

If you start at ₹1,000/month and increase by just 10% each year, your SIP grows with your income. After 20 years at 12% return, a step-up SIP grows to approximately ₹19.7 lakh — nearly 2× the flat SIP corpus of ₹9.99 lakh.
