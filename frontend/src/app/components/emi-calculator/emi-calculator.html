How EMI Calculation Works

EMI is calculated using:

EMI = P × r × (1+r)ⁿ / ((1+r)ⁿ − 1)

Where P = principal, r = monthly rate, n = total months.

Example

₹5,00,000 loan at 8.5% for 5 years → Monthly EMI: ₹10,253, Total Interest: ₹1,15,180.
