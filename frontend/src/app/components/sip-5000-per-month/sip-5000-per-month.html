Why ₹5,000/Month SIP Works for Indian Investors

At just ₹167/day, a ₹5,000 SIP is one of the most effective ways to build wealth in India. Two powerful forces work in your favour:

  • Compounding: Your returns generate further returns. In a 20-year SIP, over 75% of your final wealth comes from compounding — not your contributions.
  • Rupee Cost Averaging: Monthly investing buys more units when markets fall and fewer when they rise, averaging out your purchase cost over market cycles.
  • Salary alignment: ₹5,000/month is achievable for most employed Indians and grows proportionally with salary via step-up SIP.

₹5,000 Step-Up SIP — The Crorepati Path

A step-up SIP increases your monthly contribution by 10% each year, aligning with salary hikes. Starting at ₹5,000 with 10% step-up at 12% return:

  • After 15 years: ~₹55 lakh (vs ₹25 lakh without step-up)
  • After 20 years: ~₹1.89 crore (vs ₹50 lakh without step-up)
  • After 25 years: ~₹4.2 crore — clear crorepati territory
