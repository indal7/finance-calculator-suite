How to Save Interest on a ₹50 Lakh Loan

  1. Make 20%+ down payment: Lower principal, lower EMI.
  2. Improve credit score: 750+ often gets better rates.
  3. Prepay annually: Even one extra EMI each year can reduce tenure.
  4. Consider balance transfer: If rate difference is meaningful after fees.
  5. Increase EMI with salary hikes: Avoid carrying high interest for 25 to 30 years.
