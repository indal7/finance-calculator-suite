How SIP Calculation Works

The SIP future value is calculated using the formula:

FV = P × ((1+r)ⁿ − 1) / r × (1+r)

Where P = monthly amount, r = monthly rate, n = total months.

Example

₹5,000/month at 12% p.a. for 10 years → Total invested: ₹6,00,000, estimated corpus: ₹11,61,695.
