About Finance Calculator India

A practical, independent platform built for Indian investors who want clear, accurate numbers before making important financial decisions. No bias. No selling products. Just honest tools and helpful guides.

Published: 30 March 2026

Last updated: 30 March 2026

The Problem We're Solving

Financial planning in India is broken. Most people make major money decisions based on incomplete information:

  • No clarity on numbers: "How much will ₹1000/month grow in 10 years?" is a basic question, but most people don't know the answer or use random guesses.
  • Conflicting advice: Banks, financial advisors, and social media give different rates and timelines, creating confusion about what's realistic.
  • Hidden assumptions: Most online calculators don't explain their formulas, assumptions, or tax implications. Users are left guessing if the output is correct.
  • Bias in recommendations: Many platforms recommend products to make commissions, not because they're best for the user.
  • Language barriers: Finance terminology is intimidating. Most Indians avoid calculators because they seem too technical.

This website exists to solve these problems. We believe every Indian investor deserves access to accurate, unbiased financial planning tools — for free.

Why This Website Was Created

This journey started with conversations with friends and colleagues. When they asked questions like:

  • "If I invest ₹5000 per month for 20 years, how much will I have?"
  • "My loan EMI is ₹25,000. Is it too much for my budget?"
  • "Should I choose FD or mutual fund SIP for my ₹5 lakh?"

...most of them didn't have reliable answers. Some used old spreadsheets. Some guessed. Some were misled by social media "financial gurus." The result: poor financial decisions that cost them years of wealth building.

I built this website because I realized the problem wasn't lack of knowledge — it was lack of accessible, easy-to-use tools. A finance student in Delhi should have the same access to accurate calculators as a wealthy investment banker. And that tool shouldn't cost money or require login.

What Makes Us Different

✓ 100% Independent

We are not owned by any bank, mutual fund house, insurance company, or lending platform. There's no commission when you use a specific product. We recommend tools based on your needs, not our revenue.

✓ Completely Free

Every calculator, guide, and article is free. No hidden paywalls. No "Premium" version. No upsell to products. The only revenue model is Google AdSense, which doesn't influence our recommendations.

✓ Transparent & Explainable

Every calculator explains its formula. We show the math: EMI formula, SIP calculation logic, compound interest method. You're not using a black box — you're using a tool with visible, verifiable logic.

✓ Focused on India

We understand Indian financial products: SEBI-registered mutual funds, RBI-regulated FDs, banks' EMI terms, GST on loans, TDS on savings. We don't serve generic "US investor" content.

✓ Educational, Not Preachy

We explain the "why" behind decisions without being condescending. You'll learn concepts like "Why EMI decreases principal paid?" or "When is SIP better than lumpsum?" — not just get a number.
