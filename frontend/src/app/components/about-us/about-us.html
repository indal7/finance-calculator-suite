About Us

Finance Calculator India — Empowering Smarter Financial Decisions

Finance Calculator India is a free, independent financial tools platform built specifically for Indian investors, salaried professionals, and loan seekers. Our goal is simple: make complex financial mathematics accessible to every Indian, regardless of their background in finance.

Who We Are

Finance Calculator India was founded by Indal Saroj, a software developer and personal finance enthusiast based in India. After spending years watching friends and family struggle with investment decisions — often relying on mental maths or confusing spreadsheets — Indal decided to build a dedicated platform that makes financial planning straightforward and instant.

The website is independently operated, with no affiliation to any bank, mutual fund house, insurance company, or financial advisory firm. This independence allows us to provide unbiased tools that serve the user's interest alone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratise financial literacy in India. We believe that every Indian deserves access to the same quality of financial planning tools that were previously available only to those who could afford professional financial advisors.

Whether you are a first-time mutual fund investor trying to calculate your SIP returns, a home buyer planning your EMI budget, a senior citizen estimating fixed deposit income, or a trader evaluating portfolio growth with CAGR — our calculators are built for you.

What We Offer

We currently provide four core financial calculators, each designed with the Indian financial ecosystem in mind:

  • SIP Calculator: Calculate the maturity value of your Systematic Investment Plan with compounding, inflation adjustment, and year-by-year projection.
  • EMI Calculator: Instantly compute your monthly loan instalment for home loans, car loans, personal loans, and more with a full amortisation schedule.
  • FD Calculator: Estimate your Fixed Deposit maturity amount and interest income for all major Indian banks including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, and Axis.
  • CAGR Calculator: Measure the compound annual growth rate of any investment to evaluate and compare performance across time horizons.

All calculations are performed in real-time on your device. We do not store your financial data on any server.

Our Commitment to Accuracy

Every calculator on this platform is built using standard financial formulas endorsed by regulators like SEBI (Securities and Exchange Board of India) and the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). We regularly review and update our formulas to reflect changes in tax laws, interest rate conventions, and compounding practices used by Indian financial institutions.

While we strive for the highest level of accuracy, our calculators are intended for educational and planning purposes only. Actual returns and costs may vary based on market conditions, individual fund performance, and terms offered by specific financial institutions.

Why Trust Us?

We understand that trust must be earned. Here is why thousands of Indian investors rely on our platform:

  • No Registration Required: Use all calculators instantly without creating an account or sharing personal details.
  • No Hidden Charges: Every tool on this website is completely free, with no premium tiers or paywalls.
  • Privacy Respected: We do not store your financial inputs. All calculations happen locally in your browser.
  • Mobile-First Design: Our tools are optimised for smartphones, so you can plan on the go — whether you are at your bank, a mutual fund distributor's office, or at home.
  • India-Specific: Our calculators use Indian conventions — rupee (₹) currency, Indian compounding frequencies, and rate assumptions based on the Indian market.

Our Vision for the Future

Upcoming additions include a PPF Calculator (Public Provident Fund), NPS Calculator (National Pension Scheme), Income Tax Calculator, and a Loan Comparison Tool.
