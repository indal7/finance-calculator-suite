Published: 30 March 2026

Why This Website Was Created

This website started with one repeated problem: people around me were making loan and investment decisions using rough guesses. Sometimes they used random social posts, sometimes they used old spreadsheet files. The result was confusion.

I wanted one place where anyone in India can quickly check numbers for monthly investing, fixed deposits, and EMI planning without complicated finance language.

Who Is This For

Primary audience

Indian salaried professionals, first-time investors, families planning goals, and students who are beginning personal finance.

Problems this solves

  • How much to invest monthly in SIP for a future target.
  • How much maturity amount to expect in an FD.
  • How EMI affects monthly budget before taking a loan.
  • How to compare savings options in a practical way.

Founder Note

I am Indal Saroj, a software developer who likes building useful tools for everyday people. I am not trying to create a complicated finance portal. My focus is simple: clear calculators, understandable examples, and honest limitations.

This platform is independent. It is not owned by any bank, fund house, or lending brand.
